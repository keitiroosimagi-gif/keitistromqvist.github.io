About Me

Keiti Strömqvist is a Swedish-Estonian mezzosoprano who has performed as a soloist in works by Mozart, Haydn, Handel, and Bach. She has sung leading roles in opera productions across Sweden, Denmark, and Estonia.

Currently a member of Göteborgs Symfoniker's vocal ensemble, Keiti is pursuing her master's in opera at the University of Gothenburg.

Recent Performances

  • Carmen – Fusion of Bizet & Luup
  • Angelina in La Cenerentola
  • Cherubino in Figaro's Wedding
  • Auntie in Peter Grimes
  • Guest soloist: PULSAR Festival, Göteborgs Stadsteater
